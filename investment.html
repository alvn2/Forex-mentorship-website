Risk Management Strategies
  1. Diversification: Spread investments across asset classes, industries, and geographic regions to minimize single-point failure risks.
  2. Dynamic Asset Allocation: Regularly adjust portfolio composition based on market conditions and risk tolerance.
  3. Stop-loss Orders: Set automated sell orders to limit losses if individual asset prices drop below predetermined levels.
  4. Cash Reserves: Maintain a buffer of readily available cash to cover unexpected expenses or capitalize on investment opportunities.
